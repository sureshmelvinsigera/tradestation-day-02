# Exercise 04 – Product Innovation with AI
**Slides:** 43–44 | **Time:** 5 min | **Tool:** Microsoft Copilot

---

## 🎯 Objective
Use AI to rapidly generate, score, and visualize product ideas for a specific industry — mimicking the research finding that AI-generated ideas outperformed student ideas in purchase intent.

---

## 📎 Resources Needed
- Microsoft Copilot (free version is fine)
- Your own industry context, or pick one from the list below:
  - Financial services / trading
  - Healthcare
  - Retail / e-commerce
  - Education
  - Logistics / supply chain

---

## 💬 Solution Prompt

```
Generate 15 distinct product ideas for [YOUR INDUSTRY] professionals — 
these can be physical products or software tools.

For each idea:
- Give it a clear product name
- Write a one-sentence description
- Score it 1–10 on three dimensions:
    • Feasibility (how realistic is it to build today?)
    • Competition (1 = crowded market, 10 = wide open space)
    • Success Potential (likelihood of strong customer adoption)
- Write a one-sentence justification for each score

After listing all 15 ideas, calculate a total score for each and rank them 
from highest to lowest overall potential.

Then take the top 4 ranked ideas and create a photorealistic concept image 
for each one — visualizing what the product would look like in use.
```

---

## 🎯 Adapting the Prompt to TradeStation
Replace `[YOUR INDUSTRY]` with something like:

```
...for retail traders and self-directed investors who use an online 
brokerage platform (physical or software tools)
```

Or get more specific:
```
...for options traders who need to manage risk in real time
```

---

## ✅ What Good Output Looks Like
- 15 genuinely distinct ideas — not just variations of the same concept
- Scores that actually differ between ideas (not all 7s and 8s)
- Justifications that reference real market conditions or customer pain points
- Top 4 concept images that look like product renders, not stock photos

---

## 🔁 Follow-Up Prompts (If Time Allows)

**Dig deeper on one idea:**
```
Take idea #1 from your list. Write a one-page product brief that includes:
- The problem it solves
- The target customer
- Key features (3–5)
- Go-to-market approach
- Top 3 risks
```

**Competitive scan:**
```
For the top-ranked idea, identify 3 existing products or competitors in this 
space. What would make our version different or better?
```

---

## ⚠️ Watch Out For
- If Copilot gives "Load failed" on image generation — hit Regenerate
- Ideas may cluster around obvious solutions — push back: *"Can you give me 5 more that are more unconventional or contrarian?"*

---

## 🗣 Reflection Questions
1. Were any ideas surprising or genuinely novel to you?
2. Did the scoring feel accurate, or did the model inflate certain dimensions?
3. How would you use this workflow in your actual product or strategy work?
